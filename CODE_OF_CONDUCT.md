# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors and maintainers are expected to keep Mochi Social collaboration respectful, focused, and safe for guild members.

Expected behavior:

- Use welcoming and clear language.
- Keep feedback specific to the work.
- Respect different experience levels and communication styles.
- Avoid sharing private member, account, or security information.
- Keep the closed playtest no-real-value and member-safe.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private member, account, or security information.
- Deliberate disruption of issues, pull requests, or release checks.
- Attempts to bypass authentication, tester access, repository protections, or safety boundaries.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, close, or remove contributions that violate this code. Repeated or serious violations may result in loss of repository access or guild-facing collaboration privileges.
